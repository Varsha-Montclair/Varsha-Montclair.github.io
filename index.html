03 / Projects

Selected work.

01

Token Optimizer

A lightweight, zero-dependency pipeline reducing LLM token usage 30–75% in code generation tasks. Built a PreCall layer combining rule-based compression, semantic deduplication, and few-shot pruning for 38–42% input savings, plus semantic and exact caching for a 60% hit rate and 45–47% cost savings. Added prefix/KV caching and a configurable brevity mode, cutting stable prompt costs to roughly 10% and output tokens by ~18%, validated model-agnostically on HumanEval/MBPP.

02

Sentiment Analysis for AI News Headlines

Collaborative research with team Ivoryneurons (RAISE 2026). Analyzed 10,000+ AI news headlines from June–September 2025 using BERTopic modeling, LLM-based interpretation, and TF-IDF to track shifts in public perception of AI. Uncovered a "Trust Gap" — high optimism for AI in healthcare and education, but real concern around employment, ethics, and social integration — and found emotionally charged policy discussions peak on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
